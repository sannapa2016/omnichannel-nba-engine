Strategic Value: Omnichannel NBA Engine
1. The Business Challenge
In a crowded market (e.g., Immunology or SGLT2 inhibitors), "Share of Voice" is often wasted. Sales reps frequently visit low-potential HCPs, while high-potential targets are ignored by digital channels. This leads to high Cost Per Interaction (CPI) and stagnant market share.

2. The Analytical Solution
This NBA Engine utilizes a Multi-Class Random Forest classifier to optimize the marketing mix. By analyzing 5+ engagement variables, the model identifies the "Path of Least Resistance" for each HCP.

3. Estimated ROI & KPIs
By deploying this engine, the Commercial Excellence team can target:

Operational Efficiency: 15% reduction in travel costs by optimizing Rep visits for high-probability "conversion" targets.

Engagement Lift: 20% increase in email open rates by pivoting non-responders to webinars or direct mail.

Prescription Retention: Estimated $1.2M - $3.5M incremental net revenue per region by preventing "Share of Voice" decay in high-decile accounts.
